ABOUT US




Welcome to Foodbazaar! My name is Jenny, and I created Foodbazaar to celebrate how nourishing, fun, and delicious cooking with seasonal fruits and vegetables can be. I hope that the recipes here inspire you to try cooking a new vegetable, or try cooking a familiar vegetable in a new way.

I’m a food lover, number cruncher, and meticulous budgeter. I love science and art, and the way they come together when I cook. I love to create, problem solve, and learn new things. Making great food is my passion, my purpose, and my favorite thing to share with others.

THE TEAM


Karina is a recipe developer and author of many cookbooks. Her veggie-centric recipes celebrate how nourishing, fun, and delicious cooking with seasonal, plant-based ingredients can be. Karina has also developed recipes for KitchenAid, Whole Foods, Le Creuset, Anthropologie, and Crate & Barrel, among others. In addition, her work has appeared in Bon Appétit, People, O: The Oprah Magazine, Cooking Light, Self, Shape, HGTV, mindbodygreen, Food52, and more.

Karan is the Senior Editor and Recipe Developer for Foodbazaar. Depending on the day, he might be writing, copyediting, testing/developing recipes, or shooting blog photos with Jenny. Karan loves finding new ways to cook with seasonal vegetables, a passion he discovered during a summer he spent as a farmhand on Two Onion Farm in Belmont, Wisconsin. In his spare time, Karan writes recipes for a local CSA and volunteers with her neighborhood food pantry and community kitchen.
