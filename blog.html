Creamy Noodles

Feel free to use low-fat cream cheese, milk and sour cream in these Creamy Noodles without sacrificing flavor. I also recommend using salt-free Cajun seasoning so you can control the saltiness of the noodles. As I said, we love these Creamy Noodles. They are a favorite side dish all year round. They go with just about everything and only take a few minutes to make. Give these a try the next time you need a quick and delicious accompaniment to your meal!

prep time

30 min

cook time

20 min

servings

3 servings



Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions, and drain.

2. While spaghetti cooks, prepare the sauce.

3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, saut√© garlic in butter for 1 minute. Add the cream cheese, sour cream, milk, Cajun seasoning, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to low. Cook and stir just until smooth (do not boil). Remove from the heat and stir in parmesan cheese.

4. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. Sprinkle with parsley.
